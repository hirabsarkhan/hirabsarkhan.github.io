Interesting Facts About Dolphins

  • Dolphins are part of the family of whales that includes orcas and pilot whales.
  • There are 36 species of marine dolphins.
  • Dolphins are known to exhibit complex social behavior and can communicate with each other using a variety of clicks, whistles, and other vocalizations.
  • Dolphins have been observed using tools and passing down knowledge from generation to generation.
  • Their diet primarily consists of fish and squid.
